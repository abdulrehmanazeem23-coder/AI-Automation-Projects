REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Government of Pakistan – National IT Board

PROJECT TITLE: Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System for Federal Ministry

ISSUING AUTHORITY: National IT Board, Ministry of IT & Telecom
SUBMISSION DEADLINE: 30 August 2026
ESTIMATED BUDGET: PKR 85 Million

1. BACKGROUND
The Federal Ministry requires a comprehensive ERP system to manage HR, Finance, Procurement, and Asset Management modules across 12 departments.

2. MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
- The vendor MUST hold ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 27001 certifications.
- The vendor SHALL have completed at least 3 similar ERP projects for government clients.
- The proposed solution MUST be cloud-hosted with 99.9% uptime SLA.
- All data MUST be stored on local servers within Pakistan in compliance with data sovereignty laws.
- The vendor MUST provide a dedicated project manager with PMP certification.
- Source code MUST be handed over to the Ministry upon project completion.

3. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- Technical Approach and Methodology: 35%
- Relevant Experience and Past Projects: 25%
- Team Qualifications and CVs: 20%
- Financial Proposal / Price: 20%

4. SCOPE OF WORK
Vendors are required to respond to the following sections:
- Describe your proposed ERP architecture and technology stack.
- Provide case studies of at least 3 completed government ERP deployments.
- Submit CVs of the Project Manager and Lead Developer.
- Provide a detailed implementation timeline (maximum 18 months).
- Describe your post-go-live support and maintenance plan.

5. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
Proposals must be submitted in hard copy (3 copies) and soft copy (USB drive) to the National IT Board office by the deadline.
